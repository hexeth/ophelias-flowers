A charming ball dahlia with soft yellow and peach tones that blend beautifully together. Also known as Bracken Sarah, this variety produces perfect spherical blooms with a warm, sunny disposition. The compact ball form makes it ideal for cutting and arrangements, with excellent vase life. A reliable bloomer that produces abundant flowers throughout the season on strong stems.
