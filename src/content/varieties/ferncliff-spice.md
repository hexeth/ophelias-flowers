A captivating decorative dahlia with complex spiced-peach and pink tones that shift in different lights. The warm, nuanced color palette makes this variety incredibly versatile in both garden and vase. Medium-sized blooms are produced prolifically on strong stems perfect for cutting. The informal decorative form adds a romantic, garden-style quality to arrangements. This reliable grower performs well in various conditions and produces abundant tubers for future seasons.
