A romantic decorative dahlia with soft, dreamy blush-pink blooms that seem to float in the garden. The gentle coloring is universally flattering and works beautifully in both garden settings and arrangements. This compact to medium grower is well-suited to smaller gardens and borders. The delicate appearance belies a tough, reliable nature that produces abundant blooms throughout the season. Perfect for creating soft, romantic garden compositions or adding a gentle touch to mixed bouquets.
